Bus Parts

We are New England’s #1 Source for used coach bus parts. We stock MCI, Vanhool, and Prevost coach buses. All of our coach buses are running and driving prior to being dismantled. We have complete coach buses for parts. Call today or look at our Bus Part Search to see what we have in stock.

Our inventory process starts once a bus arrives in our yard. Similar to our inventory process for cars, the bus will undergo what we call an entry level inventory: photos are taken, and all parts are inspected and put into our state of the art inventory management system. Buses are placed on a dismantling list where specific parts are removed for orders and the rest of the bus is processed and shredded to pieces. Once parts are put on the shelf, they are given a final inspection to ensure we exceed our guests’ expectation of used coach bus parts.

We offer competitive and fair prices on all our parts. We offer UPS and freight services for out-of-state customers. We sell engines, transmissions, body panels, axles, wheels, tires, glass, bumpers, electrical components, heating and A/C components, fuel and brake components, air and suspension components, and much more!
